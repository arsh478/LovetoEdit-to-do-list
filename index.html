    Privacy Policy

    Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains how we handle your data when you use Love to Edit.

    Data Storage

    All task data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers.

    Photo Handling

    Photos you upload are converted to base64 format and stored locally. They are never uploaded to our servers or shared with third parties.

    Third-Party Services

    We use Font Awesome for icons and Google Fonts for typography. These services may collect usage analytics as per their respective privacy policies.
